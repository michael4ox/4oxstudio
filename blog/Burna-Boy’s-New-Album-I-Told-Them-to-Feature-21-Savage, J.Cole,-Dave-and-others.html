Burna Boy’s New Album ‘I Told Them’ to Feature 21 Savage, J.Cole, Dave and others

Burna Boy is readying for the release of his seventh studio album. Just a little over a year after putting out Love, Damini, the Nigerian Afrobeats music star will be dropping his new album, I Told Them, on Thursday.

Ahead of the LP’s debut, Burna Boy took to social media to share the official tracklist. Spanning 15 songs in total, I Told Them… will contain guest features from 21 Savage, Dave, J. Cole, Byron Messia and Seyi Vibez.

Burna Boy already released his 21 Savage collaboration, the hard-hitting “Sittin’ On Top Of The World.” The artist returned just earlier today to unveil his track with Dave, “Cheat On Me.”

As for the others, fans will have to wait until Thursday to hear what else Burna Boy and his collaborators have in store. The last track on the LP, which sees him team up with Messia isn’t necessarily a collaboration but rather a remix of the Saint Kittian native’s hit single “Talibans.”

Stream Burna Boy’s new single “Cheat On Me” featuring Dave. I Told Them… is out in full on Thursday, August 24.
